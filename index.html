Rock on!

  • High Concept:Dodge the asteroids moving around the screen as long as you can.
  • Theme:Sci-fi is the most fitting theme but it's mostly about asteroids.
  • Mandated Variety:Mouse input, randomly generated asteroids, and a timer.
  • Player Experience Goals:Have fun! Try not to get frustrated if you die quickly.
  • Gameplay:Dodge rocks and survive as long as possible. You can move the Earth around as much as you want but you have no controls besides that.
  • Prototype Goal:Mouse input and constant random generation of sprites.
  • Features:Very prettty rocks and a positive attitude
  • Setting/Premise:SPAAAAAACE
  • Story:In a world where rocks are constantly flying at you at incredible speeds. You must survive the onslaught until the end of time.
  • Target Audience:Anyone who can use a mouse and see a screen.
  • Play Time:Probably no more than a minute or two.
  • Strategies:Dodge ALL the rocks!
  • Prototype Assets:Assets taken from web images: EARTH: http://www.clker.com/clipart-earth-cartoon.html ASTEROID: http://www.nbcnews.com/id/43785385/ns/technology_and_science-space/t/its-official-nasa-spacecraft-orbiting-giant-asteroid/
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:A lot of flash games use the same mechanics of dodging something using your mouse.
