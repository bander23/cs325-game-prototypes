Snitches get Stitches

  • High Concept:In this game you are playing as an organ who has to avoid getting stitched by the needles.
  • Theme:Theme is medical
  • Mandated Variety:Keyboard input, Randomness, medical drama, achiever
  • Player Experience Goals:Try to survive as long as possible
  • Gameplay:Move the heart around using the directional arrows on the keyboard. The needles on the screen will be floating around randomly and at random speeds. The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible.
  • Prototype Goal:This will be using keyboard input to control the player character. And will test collisions and random movements.
  • Features:A desire to see how long you can last and if you can beat your last score.
  • Setting/Premise:You are inside a body and you are trying to avoid getting removed and placed into another persons body.
  • Story:No story. Just a fun game to play.
  • Target Audience:Anybody that can use a computer is welcome to play this game.
  • Play Time:On average under one minute.
  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game?
